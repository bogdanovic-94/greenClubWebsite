GreenUp, The Group Of Young Scouts Greeting The Nature

Young Nature Scouts is a program designed for children ages 7 to 15 to explore and appreciate their natural environment. It's a great way to get kids outdoors, learning and having fun at the same time. Through fun activities, games, and exploration, children learn how to respect and protect nature.

Various Outdoor Activities

They explore the world around them, discovering the wonders of nature and learning how to be good stewards of the earth. They also learn about the importance of preserving our natural resources and protecting wildlife. Through the program, children learn to appreciate the beauty of nature and the importance of keeping our environment safe and healthy.

Practical Knowledge

Through the program, children learn to appreciate the beauty of nature and the importance of keeping our environment safe and healthy. Young Nature Scouts teaches children about the interconnectedness of all living things, and how our actions can have a positive or negative impact on the environment. By teaching children the importance of conservation, we can help them become responsible stewards of the earth and our natural resources.
